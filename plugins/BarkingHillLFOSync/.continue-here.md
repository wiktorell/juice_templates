# Resume Point

## Current State: Stage 2 DSP Phase 3.2 Complete

MIDI clock sync and phase retrigger implemented. AudioPlayHead (JUCE 8 API) queries ppqPosition
each processBlock; when sync=true and transport is playing, lfoPhase is derived from ppqPosition
locked to the selected subdivision (13-step table, 4 BAR to 1/32T). When transport is stopped,
phase is frozen. When retrigger=true, lfoPhase resets to 0.0 on the first MIDI note-on in the
buffer. Retrigger overrides sync-derived phase on note-on events. Audio passthrough unchanged.
Ready to proceed to Stage 2 DSP Phase 3.3.
